Project 1: Balloon Shooter Game

Play Game

Only for Desktop

Features:

  • Interactive Balloons: Players can interact with the balloons, aiming to pop them by clicking on the screen.
  • Dynamic Crosshair: Stay on target with the dynamic crosshair located at the center of the screen.
  • Score Tracking: Keep track of your progress through a dedicated score meter displayed on the screen. Successfully popping balloons adds points to your score, while missing balloons results in score deductions.
  • Gameplay Challenges: To elevate the gaming experience, the balloons move in a wobbly pattern, creating an extra layer of challenge. Players must time their shots accurately to pop the balloons amidst their unpredictable movement.

How to Play:

Popping Balloons: Use your skills to pop balloons by clicking the left mouse button.
